(Public Domain)**. No attribution is required, but credit is given here as good practice.

## Everything else

All other characters use hand-authored vector (SVG) art generated in
`client/src/characters/CharacterArt.js`, drawn in a flat-toon style to match the
Kenney sprites. The original procedural Phaser drawing (`CharacterGraphics.js`)
remains as an automatic fallback.
